Privacy Policy & AI Usage Terms

Oryzed Effective Date: 1st April 2026

At Oryzed, we build AI for the legal domain. We understand that in the legal field, confidentiality and data protection are paramount. This Policy explains exactly what data we collect, how we protect it, and your rights regarding your information.

Our core promise: Your data is yours. We do not sell your Personal Data, and we process it strictly in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the SPDI Rules, 2011.

Part 1: How We Handle Your Data

1. The Data We Collect

To provide our Services, we collect specific types of information. We practice data minimization, meaning we only collect what we absolutely need.

  • Information You Provide: Your name, email, phone number, account credentials, and payment/billing details.
  • Your Content (Prompts & Documents): The queries, prompts, and legal documents you upload or interact with on our platform.
  • Sensitive Personal Data: Financial information (like payment details) and authentication data. We only collect this with your explicit, affirmative consent.
  • Automated Data: Your IP address, device/browser details, and usage logs to keep our platform secure and functional.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (via IP) for security. We will never track your precise location without your explicit consent.

2. Why We Process Your Data

We process your data strictly to:

  • Deliver, maintain, and secure the Oryzed platform.
  • Personalize your user experience.
  • Improve our AI’s performance, safety, and features.
  • Comply with Indian legal obligations and prevent fraud or misuse.

3. Who We Share Your Data With (And Who We Don't)

We never sell your personal data to data brokers or third parties for marketing. We only share data with:

  • Trusted Service Providers: Companies that help us run our business (e.g., secure cloud hosting, payment processors). They are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: Only if strictly required by a valid legal order or law enforcement request.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data only as long as you have an active account with us.

  • The 30-Day Rule: If you delete your account or request data deletion, we will permanently delete or fully anonymize your data within 30 days.
  • Exceptions: We may retain specific data longer solely if required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or resolving ongoing disputes.

5. Your Rights as a User

Under the DPDP Act, you have absolute control over your data. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Erase/Delete your data entirely.
  • Withdraw Consent at any time (which may affect your ability to use certain features).
  • Nominate a representative to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.
  • Grievance Redressal through our dedicated officer (details below).

6. Security & Cross-Border Transfers

We treat your data, especially legal documents, with the highest security standards. We implement robust encryption, strict access controls, and secure infrastructure. If your data must be processed outside India (e.g., on global cloud servers), we ensure it is protected by legal safeguards equivalent to Indian law.

Part 2: Artificial Intelligence & Legal Disclaimers

Because Oryzed is an AI-powered legal tool, it is crucial to understand how the technology interacts with your data and its limitations.

7. AI Model Training & Your Content

We constantly strive to improve Oryzed. To do this, User Content (like prompts) may be used to train and enhance our AI systems.

  • Strict Safeguards: Whenever data is used for training, it is strictly aggregated or anonymized.
  • Confidential Legal Data: While we implement reasonable safeguards, no cloud system is impenetrable. We strongly advise users to redact highly sensitive, privileged, or personally identifiable client information before uploading documents to the platform.

8. The Nature of AI (Hallucinations)

Oryzed uses probabilistic models. This means the AI generates outputs based on patterns, not human reasoning.

  • Inaccuracies: AI outputs may contain errors, fabricated information ("hallucinations"), or outdated case law.
  • User Verification: You must independently verify all AI-generated outputs before using them in any professional capacity.
